Frequently Asked Questions

1. How do I make a train reservation?

Enter your departure and arrival cities and travel dates in the link on the Home Page, or click the 'Book you ticket' button in the site header. You can book up to 6 passengers per reservation and reserve your tickets up to 11 months in advance. Available trains will be shown on the next page. book your ticket

2. How do I cancel my reservation?

You can use Amtrak.com to cancel your reservation if you made your reservation online. If you have made modifications to your trip after booking it online, you may not be able to cancel it online. If you paid for your one-way, round-trip or multi-ride reservations with a credit card or an eVoucher, you can also cancel it on Amtrak.com, even if you made your reservations on the phone, at a Quik-Trak kiosk or with a station agent. Credit can be obtained in the form of an eVoucher for canceled reservations. Cancellation fees may apply and not all tickets are eligible for cancellation. If you have a profile on Amtrak.com, log in to see your upcoming reservations and select the reservation you want to cancel. cancel

3.How can I check the status of my train?

You can also check train status on ASHAS.com by clicking the 'Train Status' button in the site header.track your train

4.What do tickets cost?

The cost of your ticket is the rail fare, plus any accommodations you wish to add to your ticket. We don't charge additional taxes or fees. Upgrades and add-ons include Business class, First class and Sleeping Accommodation, as well as bringing your vehicle (on the Auto Train) or bike with you. More about shipping addresses.

5.How to postpone/prepone journey?

This can be done on line by cancelling the original ticket and booking a fresh ticket.More about shipping addresses.
